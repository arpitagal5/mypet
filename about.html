Our Mission

Every day, we create a Second Act for ElderPups, welcoming each dog to a loving and caring home for the rest of his or her life. We track each dog through life, ensuring quality of care.

Company History

Top Dog's care platform in India managed by staff and volunteers. We do not shelter or house rescued dogs but prospective approved adopters may enjoy a personal visit with the dog under our care at a foster home or other site


The Dog Team

This work is all done by people who love dogs and want to volunteer their time and energy. We need committed support from volunteers: anywhere from a few hours a month to more responsibility that requires more time.
